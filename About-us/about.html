Our Story
"Every entrepreneur deserves the same access to government support — regardless of their education or connections."

DreamMore was founded with a single mission — to democratize access to government schemes, funding, and business support for India's millions of micro, small, and medium enterprises. Based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, we bridge the gap between ambitious entrepreneurs and the complex world of government procedures, banking systems, and compliance requirements.
