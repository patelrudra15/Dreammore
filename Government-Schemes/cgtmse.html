Is the entire loan guaranteed by CGTMSE?

No. Typically 75–85% of the loan is guaranteed. Women entrepreneurs, SC/ST borrowers, and NER region businesses get 85% cover.

Can I apply directly to CGTMSE?

No. You apply through your bank or NBFC. The lender applies for CGTMSE guarantee on your behalf after sanctioning the loan.

Does CGTMSE work with all banks?

CGTMSE works with all scheduled commercial banks, RRBs, SFBs, and select NBFCs.

What is the guarantee fee?

Guarantee fee ranges from 0.37% to 1.35% of loan amount per year, paid by the lender. Many banks pass this to the borrower — we help you negotiate the best terms.

Can CGTMSE combine with other schemes?

Yes. CGTMSE guarantee can be used alongside Mudra loans and PMEGP loans to provide collateral-free access to larger loan amounts.
