Udyam Shakti Scheme 2026: Complete Guide for Women Entrepreneurs

Everything you need to know about India's flagship scheme for women-led MSMEs — eligibility, benefits up to ₹10 lakh, and how to apply.

P

Priya Mehta

MSME Consultant

22 Jan 2026 10 min read

India has over 20 million women-led MSMEs, yet only 27% of them access formal financing. The Udyam Shakti Scheme directly addresses this gap — providing not just funding, but a complete business growth ecosystem for women entrepreneurs registered under Udyam.

What Is the Udyam Shakti Scheme?

The Udyam Shakti Scheme is a Government of India initiative that provides integrated support to women-owned MSMEs. Unlike traditional loan schemes, it combines financial support (up to ₹10 lakh), structured mentorship, skill training, and market access — all under one umbrella.

Key Benefits

  • Financial project support up to ₹10 lakh
  • Business planning and incubation support
  • Digital marketing and brand building assistance
  • Access to government procurement portals (GeM)
  • One-on-one mentorship through the business lifecycle
  • Skill development workshops (no fee)

Eligibility Criteria

Who Can Apply?

  1. 1Must be a female entrepreneur (sole proprietor, partner, or director with 51%+ stake)
  2. 2Business must be registered under Udyam (MSME registration)
  3. 3Enterprise must be in the Micro or Small category
  4. 4Business should have growth potential and a defined business plan

How to Apply: Step-by-Step

  1. 1Obtain Udyam Registration (free, online at udyamregistration.gov.in)
  2. 2Prepare your Business Plan or DPR (Detailed Project Report)
  3. 3Submit application through MSME Development Institute (MSME-DI) in your district
  4. 4Attend orientation session and eligibility interview
  5. 5Post-approval: begin training modules and access mentorship network

Expert Tip

Most applications are rejected due to an incomplete DPR (Detailed Project Report). A professionally written DPR that clearly outlines market opportunity, financial projections, and employment generation dramatically increases approval chances.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I apply if my business is less than 1 year old?

Yes. New businesses are eligible as long as they are registered under Udyam and have a viable business plan. In fact, the incubation support is specifically designed for early-stage businesses.

Is the ₹10 lakh support a grant or a loan?

It is a combination — a portion is a subsidized soft loan, and a portion may be a direct grant depending on your category (SC/ST women get higher grant portions). DreamMore can help you structure your application to maximize the grant component.

Conclusion

The Udyam Shakti Scheme is one of the most comprehensive support systems available to women entrepreneurs in India. If you are a women business owner with a registered MSME, this scheme can provide the financial boost and structured support you need to scale. The key is a strong application — something our team specializes in.
