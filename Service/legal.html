Why should I register my trademark?

Trademark registration gives you exclusive rights to your brand name and logo in India. Without it, anyone can copy your brand. You can use the ® symbol only after registration.

What is the difference between copyright and trademark?

Trademark protects brand identifiers (name, logo). Copyright protects creative works (books, software, music). Patent protects inventions.

What happens if I miss ROC compliance deadlines?

Late ROC filings attract penalties of ₹100–200 per day per form. Continued default can lead to director disqualification and company strike-off.

What contracts does every MSME need?

At minimum: Partnership Deed / SHA, Vendor Agreement, Client Agreement, Employment Offer Letters, and NDA.
